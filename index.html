Frequently Asked Questions

What grooming services do you offer?

We offer full grooming, de-shedding treatment, de-matting, flea & tick treatment, tangle re-opening, and medicated shampoo services for all breeds.

How often should I bring my pet for grooming?

It depends on your pet's breed and coat type. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is recommended for most dogs, but we can provide personalized recommendations.

Do you provide supplements for pets?

Yes! We offer a range of supplements including Blood Powder, Vitamin C, Salmon Oil, Omega-3, Rehydration, Multiflex, and Kelp supplements.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Now" button or contacting us directly. We'll work with your schedule to find the perfect time.
